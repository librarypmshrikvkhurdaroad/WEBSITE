About the Library

The school library serves as a vibrant learning hub for students and staff, supporting academic excellence, curiosity, and creativity. With a growing collection of fiction, non-fiction, reference materials, digital resources, and subject-specific books, the library caters to students across all classes and academic streams. It is a quiet space where learners develop reading habits, conduct research, and explore new ideas.

The library operates with the goal of creating an environment that encourages self-learning, critical thinking, and independent exploration. Students are guided on how to choose books, use reference tools, and access curated educational material for projects and assignments. Weekly library periods allow regular reading opportunities, helping students discover genres they love and build lasting connections with books.

Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality resources that foster curiosity and academic growth. We aim to nurture lifelong readers and thinkers by offering a balanced collection of books and digital content that supports classroom learning, personal interests, and overall knowledge enrichment.

Vision

To create a modern, student-friendly library that integrates technology with traditional reading, promotes research skills, and inspires young minds to explore beyond textbooks. We strive to develop responsible digital citizens and informed individuals who are prepared for the challenges of the 21st century.

Facilities

  • Well-organized shelves with fiction, non-fiction, reference books, and textbooks
  • Dedicated reading area for students and teachers
  • Computer access for digital learning and research
  • Library automation system for easy book search and issue
  • Newspaper corner and magazine section for current affairs
  • Quiet and supervised environment for focused study

Library Activities

The library regularly organizes literacy-based activities to enhance reading interest and creative thinking. Events include:

  • Book fairs and exhibitions
  • Reading challenges and book review competitions
  • Storytelling sessions for younger students
  • Workshops on research and reference skills
  • Reader of the Month recognition

Library Rules & Guidelines

  • Maintain silence inside the library at all times
  • Books should be handled with care
  • Borrowed books must be returned on or before the due date
  • Reference books and periodicals must be read inside the library
  • Use computers only for educational purposes

The school library is committed to supporting every learner’s academic journey. By offering a rich collection of books, organized facilities, and a peaceful learning environment, it stands as an essential pillar of our educational ecosystem.
